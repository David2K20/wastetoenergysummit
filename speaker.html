Ezekiel Egboye

Speaker

Founding Executive and Chief Operating Officer at Rack centre. Under his leadership, Rack Centre has become a leader in the industry with global recognition for delivery and quality excellence.
Prior to Rack Centre, Ezekiel was the Chief Operating Officer for Dimension Data West Africa where he championed the West African operations through a complex operational and financial crisis.

Jide Onokoya

Speaker

Chief operating officer at Elektron - a developer of prime energy infrastructure in West Africa.
Prior to Elektron, Jide Onakoya was a Senior Business Development Manager at Axxela, where he was responsible for for driving business growth by championing new business opportunities, capital investment initiatives, operational improvements and strategic alliances across power and gas infrastructure.

Dr. Muyiwa Gbadegesin

Speaker

Dr. Muyiwa Gbadegesin is a scientist, consultant, and public administrator with deep and varied experience. He served as an advisor to two Lagos state Governors and held roles in Oyo State Government as special Adviser, Commisioner for Health and Deputy Chief of Staff to late Governor Abiola Ajimobi.
During his time in Lagos State Government, he led the restructuring of the Property Identification Exercise (PIE) and Land Use Charge (LUC) billing system, expanding the PIE database to 250,000 properties and doubling revenue to NGN350 million in the first year.
