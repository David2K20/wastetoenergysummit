Ezekiel Egboye

Speaker

Founding Executive and Chief Operating Officer at Rack centre, where he manages the business operations and leads a team of 30 employees. Under his leadership, Rack Centre has become a leader in the industry with global recognition for delivery and quality excellence. He took the business from start-up in 2013 to full-scale operations, and led the strategic development, integration, and operations of the first Carrier Neutral Tier III constructed co-location Data Centre facility in Sub-Saharan Africa.
Prior to Rack Centre, Ezekiel Egboye was the Chief Operating Officer for Dimension Data West Africa where he championed the West African operations through a complex operational and financial crisis.
Ezekiel Egboye has over 20 years in the UK and Canada as a technology infrastructure expert, successfully implementing and managing numerous multi-million-dollar technology projects for major fortune 500 companies including IBM, Toronto Dominion Bank, Royal Bank of Canada, Sun Life Financial, Rogers Telecommunications.
He is a board member of the National Executive Council – Association of Telecommunications Companies of Nigeria (ATCON).

Jide Onakoya

Speaker

Chief Operating Officer at Elektron - a developer of prime energy infrastructure in West Africa. Jide Onakoya has over 20 years of experience in the energy and thermal power sector spans project execution, proposal development, project controls, process management, business management and project development.
Before Joining Elektron, He was a Senior Manager a gas infrastructure group where he was responsible for driving business growth by championing new business opportunities, capital investment initiatives, operational improvements and strategic alliances across power and gas infrastructure.

Dr. Muyiwa Gbadegesin

Speaker

Dr. Muyiwa Gbadegesin is a scientist, consultant, and public administrator with deep and varied experience. He served as an advisor to two Lagos State governors (Asiwaju Bola Ahmed Tinubu and Mr. Babatunde Fashola) and held roles in Oyo State Government as Special Adviser, Commissioner for Health, and Deputy Chief of Staff to late Governor Abiola Ajimobi.
During his time in Lagos State Government, he led the restructuring of the Property Identification Exercise (PIE) and Land Use Charge (LUC) billing system, expanding the PIE database to 250,000 properties and doubling revenue to NGN350 million in the first year.

Victor Abimbola Onifade

Speaker

Victor Abimbola Onifade is an urban and regional planning specialist with interests in housing and user’s satisfaction, urban management, internally displaced persons and environmental management. He holds a PhD in Urban and Regional Planning from the University of Ibadan, Ibadan, Nigeria.
He is currently a Senior Lecturer of urban planning, and teaches courses relating to housing, Regional Planning, National development planning and Sustainable urban management. He is the Cluster Manager of Sustainable Cities and African Urbanization Dynamics, Pro-Poor Development Research cluster member at the Centre for Housing and Sustainable Development, University of Lagos, He has won the University of Lagos Central Research Grant award in 2016 and a Post-doctoral Researcher, Informal Appropriation of public space and urban infrastructure for Leisure Physical Activity in Lagos and Yaoundé (ALPhA) - Funded by British Academy – Infrastructures of Wellbeing program 2020-2021.
A Post – doctoral Researcher, NIHR Global Health Research Group on Diet and Activity- A Syndemic approach to the prevention of diet and physical activity related NCDs project 2022-2023 and also, a Named Researcher: Growing up in African Cities: Focus on Low Income Communities in Lagos, Nigeria- Funded by Lagos African Cluster Centre, Institute of African and Diaspora Studies, University of Lagos 2022-2023. He is a registered town planner and a member of Nigerian Institute of Town Planners.
