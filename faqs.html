Who can participate in the Waste to Energy Summit?

Everyone! The summit welcomes participation from a diverse range of stakeholders including government representatives, private sector entities, NGOs, academia, as well as communities and residents interested in contributing to a sustainable Lagos.

To stay updated on sumit announcements, news, and updates, please follow our official social media channels. You can also check our website regularly for the latest information.

Yes, there will be virtual attendance options available for those unable to attend in person. Details on how to access the virtual sessions will be provided closer to the summit dates.
